TEDxCBIT 2018

Beyond Prejudice

Chitra talks how prejudice hinders the ability of a person to think more that themselves. Using real world examples from her life, how prejudice suppressed a large part her daughters life.

And how overcoming prejudice and thinking beyond it would make peoples life beautiful.
